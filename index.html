❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is ShopSphere?
ShopSphere is a Telegram marketplace bot where you can open a shop, buy and sell products with escrow protection, and pay using Telegram Stars. It supports 35+ countries and 23 languages.
Is ShopSphere free to use?
Yes! Browsing and buying is completely free. Opening a shop costs 10 Stars (5 for Premium). New users get 5 free Stars and can use promo code SPHERE2026 for 30 days Premium free.
How does escrow protection work?
When you buy from a seller, your payment is held in escrow until you confirm receipt. If there's a problem, you can dispute and get a refund. The platform does not hold money — all payments go directly between buyer and seller.
How do I earn real money?
You earn Telegram Stars from sales, referrals (25 Stars per invite), daily bonuses, and giveaways. Stars can be converted to real money via Fragment.com (minimum 1000 Stars, 21-day hold).
Which countries are supported?
35+ countries including Turkey, USA, UK, Germany, France, Saudi Arabia, UAE, India, Brazil, Japan, South Korea, Indonesia, and many more. Prices are auto-converted to local currency.
Is it safe?
Yes! Identity verification, escrow protection, and Telegram Stars payments ensure safety. The platform is NOT a legal intermediary — all payments are direct between buyer and seller.
How do I open a shop?
Start the bot, verify your identity, tap 'Open Shop', choose a category, and add products. Shop opening costs just 10 Stars (5 for Premium).
